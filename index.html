Easy Features

Yummy Search is a tool that enables  you to easily search, get info, order, pay and review food trucks near you. Here are four major features that Yummy Search can do!

Search

You can search truck on map by entering zip code, address, keywords, or by your current location. You can see food trucks on the map view or list view.

Overview

You can click to open detailed food truck information including menus and reviews. You can also make a call or share to your firiends.

Order

From the menu, you can see full items with picture and price. From there, you can easily select your favorite foods to make a order to pick up later.

Pay

You can choose to pay by credit card, Apple Pay or cash. Yummy Search can remember your information so that you can pay easily the next time you use it.
